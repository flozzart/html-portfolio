The Best Series According To Me

My Top 3 Series Of All Time

Young Sheldon

A hilarious and heartwarming look into the childhood of Sheldon Cooper! This show perfectly captures the struggles and brilliance of a young genius growing up in a small town, with plenty of laughs and touching family moments.

The Good Doctor

An inspiring medical drama that follows Dr. Shaun Murphy, a young surgeon with autism and savant syndrome, as he navigates the challenges of the medical world. Emotional, gripping, and full of life-changing moments!

The Big Bang Theory

A legendary sitcom that brings geek culture to life! With its witty humor, lovable characters, and endless science jokes, this show is a must-watch for anyone who loves comedy and nerdy fun.
